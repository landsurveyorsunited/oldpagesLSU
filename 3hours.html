Who Represents the Collective Voice of Surveyors in Your Area of the World?

Do you even know?  Is that voice identifiable online? Is it something that you can say you belong to?  Do you feel like your personal-professional voice is being taken into consideration when surveyors in your area are represented?  Is the surveying industry in your area collective represented on a global scale? Most likely, the general public in your geographic region has no idea as to the importance of a professional land surveyor.  I say this because it is something that the vast majority of us share in common world-wide.  This needs to change and it is up to us to turn this situation around.  Luckily, this seed of change has been embedded into the mission and goals of Land Surveyors United since 2007.  However, it requires a small amount of effort on your part. Our primary mission in building this community has always been to have Surveying Related discussions, photos, education and representation for all surveyors in every location on earth.  For this very reason, every group forum on the network has within it a dedicated forum specifically for that location.  With that said, a membership in this community is a pillar of strength for the industry as a whole, globally, but most importantly, this strenth is supported and promoted on the local level.    join your area's hub and start a discussion/share relevant experiences/ meet other surveyors in your area. Land Surveyors United is dedicated to curating a virtual representation of the surveying industry in your location, its history, present and future of land management.
