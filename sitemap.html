Land Surveyors University
Land Surveyors University is a community social surveying education event which happens all year from June 21st (the day after Survey Earth in a Day event) and ends on June 19th. The university is comprised of TEN Levels of experience where members of Land Surveyors United are able to demonstrate their expertise for a chance to win prizes and klout in the surveying industry.
Survey Earth in a Day®
Geospatial Professionals around the world take survey grade GPS readings on June 20th @ 12 Noon in order to remeasure the earth's surface as a community. Survey Earth in a Day is a reoccuring annual event on Land Surveyors United.

Land Surveyors United is the first-of-its-kind, largest and most helpful global social support network for professional land surveyors and geospatial professionals on planet earth.  Our network features enable the common surveyor to share experiences from the field, help one another troubleshoot issues on the go using our mobile interface and educate the public about the importance of professional surveyors to civilization.  Through sharing your experiences, getting involved in community projects and expressing your opinions about industry problems, you have the ability to change the world and build your reputation online. With over a million pages on our network, you'll enjoy exploring and sharing inside an environment that is geared for surveyor support, teaching and learning and all the while making your life as a surveyor more managable and perhaps even exciting once again!  Our network is 100% maintained through member donations, which is why you see very little advertising around the site, so if you find it useful and helpful, please consider contributing to the cause.
