The Texas Land Surveyors Forum, found withing the Texas Land Surveyors Group, is a place for professional land surveyors in the state of Texas to discuss anything related to land surveying in Texas, specifically. Like many other state boards for United States Surveyor Associations, Texas was in great need of a usable forum for land surveyors to utilize for communication and for the TSPS member to discuss the surveying industry, changes in surveying laws and guidelines.  Back in 2009, Land Surveyors United provided this alternative in order to facilitate the need of professionals in Texas.  If you are a land surveyor in the state of Texas, please join and contribute to this group forum.
For all other United States Forums, please see the body of this group on the network.
