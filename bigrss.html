A Big Question:  Will We Have RSS Modules in 3.0?

Before I jump off a bridge, please someone enlighten me with answers to a few simple questions, if you will:

I have seen TONS of creators ask but can't seem to find a strait answer on this topic. Also i see that it has been added back as a feature request, but what happens to all of our existing RSS feed content and our thousands/millions of subscribers to our individual feeds?  

Will We Have RSS Modules in 3.0?  I understand that our present feeds plugged into modules will not migrate but will we still have the option of adding an RSS feed module to a page?  

Will 3.0 activity generate any RSS feed?

More importantly, Will tag based content still generate individual feeds for tags?

If this has in fact been answered please point me to the discussion, but if not, please answer these simple questions for me and the rest of the creators who are patiently waiting to be fed.

I for one feel that the ability to import tagged content into a group RSS module is one of the most attractive aspects of groups..

I hope that Ning doesn't forget that it owes a lot of its success to the ability to mix and mingle RSS feeds, but looking at this FAQ (where RSS is mentioned only once), it doesn't look like too much of a priority.

We truly NEED RSS for photo tags and video tags and forum tags and blog tags.  Without this unique-to-Ning feature, I'd consider moving to wordpress or otherwise.
