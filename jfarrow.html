Justin Farrow is the founder and principal of Land Surveyors United, the world's first ever global social network for professional land surveyors and the geospatial industry. Aside from his continuing pursuit to unite the geospatial industry in the name of support, other projects include Home Free Organization, a unique mobile support solution to homelessness, Justin Farrow Media, social reputation management for professional artists and musicians. In his free time, Justin is a street photographer, mixed media artist and blogger.

He writes about social media as it relates to solving real world problems in business and art, maintains several Twitter accounts and Facebook profiles on behalf of his friends and clients and educates all of those who he comes in contact of the ways to utilize social media to build and manage reputable online brands. In the past, you may have known him as the Loft Ninja, a renegade real estate broker in North Brooklyn, NY who worked exclusively with professional artists and those being evicted during mass gentrification to find new spaces to live and work. Justin has a BA for Philosophy & Religious Studies and a BS for Anthropology, currently finishing his Masters in Applied Cultural Anthropology. Soon, you can call him Dr. Justin Farrow upon finishing his PhD for Applied Anthropology, in NY.

Presently, you can find JFarrow bouncing between school and projects in Brooklyn, NY and his family in central Florida. He is a freelance web developer, writer, street photographer and content creator for many online projects.
