At B & B Surveying Company of Houston Texas, we are independent Professional Land Surveyors. We can provide Land Title Surveys, Standard Land Surveys, ALTA/ACSM Land Title Surveys, Topographic Surveys, Elevation Certificates, Expert Witness services, Affidavit of Merit, Subdivision Platting and Map Records, Abandonment Surveys, Variance Surveys, Consent to Encroach Surveys, and a variety of other Land Survey related services. We perform both residential and commercial Land Surveys. 

To order your free proposal for service click here.

B & B Surveying can provide our product in Autocad or PDF formats in addition to hard copies of your survey or related data. This will make it simple for you to utilize for the purposes necessary for your project. We operate under the Texas Society of Professional Surveyors Manual of Practice Standards or ALTA/ACSM Standards only. This assures you receive a high quality standardized product.

Professional Land Surveyors are not advocates for their clients. Rather they are advocates for the public good in general. Land Surveys reveal nuanced issues concerning real property ranging from title concerns to use problems. We are rated "A" in 27 out of 28 consumer reports with 1 "B" rating on Angie's list. We provide a 30 minute consultation with the Professional Land Surveyor free of charge with any product ordered.
