Introduction to This Example Hub

This is an example of a US State Hub inside Land Surveyors United.  State Groups in the new LSU have many features not found in the old site.  In this example, we will reference Wisconsin Society of Land Surveyors

Chapter Specific Forums for All Chapters inside a state-  Hub Controllers have the ability to add multiple forum pages to the hub through the dashboard, allowing each state chapter to have their own chapter specific forum.

President's Blog for Broadcasting to Entire State- Utilizing the Blog Feature in side the hub, Hub Controller can designate a Blog for the President or State Leader to keep in constant contact with surveyors of the state.

Live Streaming Chapter Meetings Using Google Hangouts API- State decisions should no longer be held up by lack of votes and low member turnout in meetings.  You can now stream your chapter meetings live to your hub.

Ability to add Chapter Meeting Minutes for Those who couldn't attend- In addition to live streaming participation, you can now blog live and or embed the latest chapter meeting minutes to your hub, in minutes.

Pages Dedicated to Promoting surveying businesses in the state- The Blog Feature can also be used to easily add a directory to your Hub, showcasing outstanding surveyors in the state and helping them promote their businesses.

State specific events and conferences with RSVP- Promote your events and increase member turnout in the soon to be released Events feature for Hubs.

Embed digital publications and newsletters - You now have the ability to embed any document type directly into any hub page without having to wait on a web developer.

Integrate all social media channels into pages, forums and blogs- With Hub Addons, we can import all of your other social media profiles and acccounts to both increase engagement and keep all social media efforts in one central place.

Photos page specific to Hub to allow members to upload photos from the field- No longer do you have to send out requests for photos.  Your members can upload them directly into your hub for promotion of your state.


 

 

Hub Leader Can Customize the Entire Hub Including the Following:

Control all images and cover photos inside group

Manage pages inside group with ability to add infinite number of pages

Designate moderators for individual forums inside group

Add custom codes and embeds to pages
