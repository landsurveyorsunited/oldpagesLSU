  • About LSU

    Because You Don't Just Love Surveying, You Live It!

    The absolute best support solution for a professional land surveyor is the advice of another experienced surveyor.

    LSU gives you a place to build a personal support network which has no language barriers and spans the entire globe.

    Ask a surveying related question, get an educated answer from a fellow surveyor! Finding answers has never been so much fun.

    Are you experienced in a specialized type of surveying? Not only can you find others working in your field, you could actually change the industry with your input!

    Land Surveyors United is Earth's First Social Support network dedicated to helping land surveyors accomplish their work in less time with the help of their peers.

    LSU is NOT your average surveying forum board.

    On LSU, our support groups are centered around a topic, a location or a particular manufacturer so that land surveyors may get the exact type of support which they are searching for.Read more...

    With professional land surveyors in virtually every location on earth, it is simple to find like minded collegues based on their location and share information in real time.Read more...

    Surveyors just like you are creating the content that drives Land Surveyors United. Nobody told them to, they just can now. On LSU, you never know how your picture of 'that snake from the woods today' might be more popular than a video tutorial about 'how to plumb a prism rod using a doorway'. The top content on LSU is a showcase of the most popular photos, videos, discussions and articles for the month or week. Featured members rise to the top through networking, sharing surveying related news and information about the industry.
